About

Founded in 2010 by H. H. Shri Vishwavallabha Theertha Swamiji of Shri Sode Vadiraja Mutt – one of the 700 plus years old Ashta Mutts associated with the world famous Shri Krishna temple of Udupi in Karnataka – Shri MadhwaVadiraja Institute of Technology & Management (SMVITM),situated at Bantakal in Udupi has carved a niche for itself in imparting quality engineering education in the coastal Karnataka region.
Shri Swamiji started the institute with the sublime objective of providing quality higher education to the needy student community at affordable costs.The motto Engineering your Career and Character with Care well reflects the objectives and philosophy of the institute. Despite, being the youngest institute in the region, SMVITM has made remarkable strides in its journey towards excellence over the past five years and has emerged as the most promising engineering institute with its well-qualified and motivated faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure and distinguished learning-centric facilities.


At present, SMVITM, with a total student intake of 441 per year, offers Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) courses in Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering Electronics & Communication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. Currently, 1562 students are pursuing their engineering education at the institute. Despite being located in a rural area, SMVITM ensures that its students are well trained in existing engineering practices and acquainted with the latest industrial trends.The accomplishments of SMVITM have already garnered well-deserved recognition and widespread praise from eminent personalities across the spectrum. Late Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, former President of India, having admired the vision and value addition to engineering education taking place at the institute, made a visit to the campus and appreciated the holistic education imparted at SMVITM and inspired the students in 2014. Shri A. S. Kiran Kumar, Chairman, ISRO; Dr. B. N. Suresh, Distinguished Scientist, ISRO; Justice Dr. M. Rama Jois and many more renowned scientists, technocrats and educationists have visited the institute to motivate and guide the students.

Owing to its exalted Vision, Mission and Objectives; commitment to the cause of education and remarkable accomplishments, SMVITM is consistently maintaining record admissions of more than 90 per cent of the intake for the last three years. Though the institution is still taking off, the achievements of its students, faculty and staff are many and commendable. The institute is growing fast, scaling greater heights by concentrating on excellence in education, research and promotion of placement & entrepreneurship through industry-academia tie-ups and value added programs. The management and the academic administration have been steadfast in their commitment to make SMVITM one of the best engineering institutes in South India in the very near future.

Some Milestones in the Journey towards Excellence:

  • Formation of External Technical Advisory Board comprising distinguished researchers and eminent professors from the USA in an attempt to create interdisciplinary Centers of Excellence and facilitating multi-faceted technological growth
  • Academic partnerships/MoU made with leading industries/organizations like SAP Labs, Bengaluru; CVC Pvt. Ltd., Bengaluru; Advanced Electronic Systems, Bengaluru; Vinayaka Engineering Works, Mangaluru; University of Agricultural & Horticultural Sciences, Shivamogga; FE Designs, Bengaluru for training/internship/project work/continuing education programs for the students, faculty and staff
  • Consistently topping in University (VTU) examination results – 100percent results recorded in both first and second outgoing batches.
  • VTU recognized Research Centers and execution of externally funded research projects in the Departments of Basic Sciences.
  • stablishment of R&D Centre funded by KCTU, Dept. of Commerce & Industry, Govt. of Karnataka on Technology Up-gradation of Cashew Processing Equipment in the Department of Mechanical Engineering
  • IT facility agreement with premier IT company Tata Consultancy Services (TCS).
  • Collaboration with CSIR, NAL and ISRO scientists for student training/outreach programs.
  • Institutional merit-cum-means scholarships and other attractive scholarships/awards instituted by benevolent well-wishers, charitable trusts and Govt.Departments.
  • Striking Placement Opportunities and Facilities – Fully furnished, spacious placement block to conduct all the processes related to campus placement drives
  • KSCST & VGST (TRIP), Govt. of Karnataka sponsored and promoted student project work having social impact
  • Effective pre-placement training and personality development programs on a regular basis
  • Grants under Seed Money to Young Scientists for Research (SMYSR) scheme of Govt. of Karnataka
  • Unique, industry promoted Innovation Lab in the E&CE Department and Creativity Center in the Mechanical Engineering Dept. and Internet of Things Club in the CS&E Dept. to identify, bring out, nurture and promote creative ideas/innovative thinking of budding engineers
  • Well-qualified and highly committed team of faculty and staff, satisfying all the statutory requirements with doctorates serving in all departments
  • Ongoing research activity: more than 27 research/review papers in well recognized national/international journals and more than 22 papers in the national/international conferences/symposiums (with in India as well as abroad) presented/published during the past one year; Many faculty members pursuing doctoral studies
  • Organization of state level, inter-collegiate, techno-cultural fests and Project Model Exhibition every year to recognize and reward talented students
